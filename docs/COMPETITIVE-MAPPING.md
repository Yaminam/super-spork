## 1. Diageo India / United Spirits Limited

**What it is.** The market leader in Indian spirits and a listed company (NSE: UNITEDSPR) majority-owned by Diageo. Two surfaces: `unitedspirits.in` (the listed-entity corporate/investor site) and `diageoindia.com` (the Diageo-branded India presence).

| Dimension | Assessment |
|---|---|
| IA & content depth | Strong on the listed-entity side — clear "About / Investors / Sustainability / Newsroom / Careers" spine. Split across two domains dilutes a single corporate narrative. |
| Corporate / ESG / governance | Mature. Board, committees, policies and an annual **ESG index** are published (reported ~93% GHG reduction since 2020, ~99% renewable electricity). |
| Investor & disclosure quality | The benchmark in India: annual reports, quarterly results, filings, shareholder info, AGM — as required of a listed entity. |
| Visual & brand expression | Competent corporate, leans on Diageo's global system. Not distinctly Indian; more functional than premium. |
| Storytelling (people/mfg/sustainability) | Good sustainability and people content; manufacturing footprint is thin and rarely a hero. |
| Mobile & CWV | Reasonable, template-driven; not a performance showcase. |
| AI search visibility | High entity authority (listed, widely cited) → surfaces well in answer engines for "largest spirits company India". |
| Schema / structured data | Organization + investor basics; limited FAQ/Article/Person depth. |
| **Does well** | Investor-grade rigour; ESG measurement and disclosure. |
| **Gap to own** | One coherent, premium, distinctly-Indian corporate story; manufacturing & agri-sourcing as narrative; answer-engine-ready content. |

## 6. Where Pernod Ricard India should position itself

**The white space we will own:** *the most premium, most credible, most AI-discoverable corporate presence in Indian alco-bev — told as a distinctly Indian story.*

Three moves the competitive set leaves open:

1. **Premium corporate editorial, not brand merchandising.** USL and ABD read functional; Radico is brand-led. We present the company — heritage, craft, governance, people — with the restraint of a luxury house, while staying surrogate-safe (no consumer cues).

2. **The Indian story as the spine.** Diageo Global is the quality bar but generic-to-India. We lead with what no one else does well: *Made in India* manufacturing (Nashik, Behror, 30+ plants), agri-sourcing and water stewardship, livelihoods, and the 1805-lineage-meets-1993-build narrative.

3. **Built for the next five years of discovery (GEO/AEO).** The whole set is weak on structured data and answer-engine readiness. We ship deep Schema.org (Organization, Person, Brand, Article, FAQ), `llms.txt`, answer-ready Q&A and freshness signals — so we are accurately represented in ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Claude and Google AI Overviews, where competitors are thin.

**What to match (table stakes):** USL-grade investor/disclosure rigour and Diageo-Global-grade governance depth. We meet the floor on these and win on premium expression, Indian narrative and AI-readiness.

**One-line positioning:** *Where the rigour of a listed leader meets the restraint of a luxury house — and a story that is unmistakably India's.*
